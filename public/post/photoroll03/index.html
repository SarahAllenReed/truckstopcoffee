Photo Roll 03 - Scenes From The Studio - The Garden Concept Art, part 1

A collection of in-progress photos from the development and illustration process for the concept art for The Garden, an upcoming comic from Cabbage and I.

photo roll / misc / comics / illustration / art / process / the garden

Part one of two sets of progress photos of me working my way through the concept art for The Garden.

The initial pencils for the concept art image. I hid [Gnome Chompski](https://combineoverwiki.net/wiki/Garden_Gnome) in there somewhere--see if you can find 'im!

Starting in on inks, going section by section.

Layer layer layer

The final, digitized inks.

Starting in on colors.

Nearing the final colors now. Tune in next time to see the finished piece!
