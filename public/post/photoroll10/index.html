Photo Roll 10 - Meet Navi!

Navi is my beloved Pinephone. This page is where you can find some glamour shots and screenshots of her.



Written and published on November 4, 2023
257 words, roughly 2 minutes worth of readin'
photo roll / misc / linux / pinephone

Part one of a series of progress photo galleries of me working through Our Lady Maven, Issue 2.

Here's the baby! Shown here with Vim and Nerdtree, with a tin of Bengal Slices and one of my many pipes.

Here's some screenshots of my original setup, starting with Vim with Startify.

Dosbox with Frederick Pohl's Gateway.

Liferea. Due to the Pinephone's smaller screen, this isn't the *best* solution for RSS feeds, but I've yet to find a CLI one I like.

And, of course, everyone's favorite PySol Fan Club Edition, which of course runs flawlessly on the Pinephone...well as long as you're not trying to make that tiny screen render an entire game of Belagured Castle like an absolute psychopath. I'm playing Eagle Wing here.

A couple months back, Navy ended up on the fritz. I ended up taking her apart, thinking it may be a failing battery...

...and in the process, wiped and re-installed a boot disk on the SD card.

Navi's shell, taking a nap while I work on the Listening List.

Turns out, no battery errors at all! It was a fairly common error where loading the OS from the SD card would end up in a boot loop. So I re-installed Postmarket directly onto the phone's memory itself, and we were good to go!

Here's the end result. First, the obligatory Neofetch shot.

Vim and Vimwiki, which my own personal incarnation of misspells as Zimwiki because that's what I used for years before stumbling upon Vimwiki.

Ranger and feh.

And the main desktop.
